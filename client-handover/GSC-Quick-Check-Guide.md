# Google Search Console Quick Check Guide
**Project**: apexengineering.org.in

---

## 📈 5-Minute Monthly Audit Workflow

Keep your SEO performance on track with this simple monthly checklist:

### Step 1: Open Google Search Console
* Navigate to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console).
* Select your property: `https://apexengineering.org.in/`.

### Step 2: Check Search Performance
* Click on **Performance** $\rightarrow$ **Search Results**.
* Compare the total clicks and impressions for the last 28 days with the previous period.
* Confirm that impressions are trending upwards (minor daily fluctuations are normal).

### Step 3: Monitor Page Indexing
* Click on **Indexing** $\rightarrow$ **Pages**.
* Verify if there are any new red error flags.
* Check if key routes (like your product detail links) are marked as "Indexed".

### Step 4: Check Core Web Vitals
* Click on **Experience** $\rightarrow$ **Core Web Vitals**.
* Ensure both Mobile and Desktop categories show green status.

### Step 5: Verify Structured Data
* Click on **Enhancements** (Product snippets, Merchant listings, FAQ, Breadcrumbs).
* Confirm that zero error codes are reported.
