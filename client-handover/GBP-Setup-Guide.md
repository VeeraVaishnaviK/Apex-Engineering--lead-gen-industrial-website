## 📍 Step-by-Step Google Maps Activation (Client Actions)

Google Business Profile (GBP) is the single most important factor for local Chennai rankings. Complete these steps within your business Google account:

### Step 1: Claim and Verify
1. Go to [Google Business Profile](https://business.google.com).
2. Sign in with your primary company Google account.
3. Search for: `Apex Engineering` (Ambattur / Rampuram address).
4. Select the profile and choose your verification method (Phone call, SMS, or video verification of your workshop facility).

### Step 2: Set Standardized Info
Ensure details match the website codebase exactly:
* **Business Name**: `Apex Engineering`
* **Address**: `1/2 Balambigai Nagar 1st Main Road, Rampuram, Chennai, Tamil Nadu 600089`
* **Phone numbers**: `+91 89253 01739` and `+91 72008 11328`
* **Business Category**:
  * *Primary*: `Steel Fabricator` or `Sheet Metal Contractor`
  * *Secondary*: `Industrial Equipment Supplier`, `Material Handling Equipment Supplier`

### Step 3: Add Media & Photos
Upload 10+ high-resolution, unedited photos of your workshop:
* **Exterior**: Workshop entrance with company board.
* **Interior**: Shop floor, welding zones, steel storage racks.
* **Products**: Custom mild steel tables, silent nylon trolleys, and roller conveyors.

### Step 4: Link Your Website
* Set the website URL strictly to: `https://apexengineering.org.in/`.
